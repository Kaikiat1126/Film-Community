WEEKLY CELEBRITY FORUM
We have a weekly celebrity forum on Wednesday evenings at 8pm, where we work with participants on the type of performance, story journey, personal characteristics and what to learn from them. The event will be a two-hour discussion format and the results will be uploaded to our website. A link to the participation forum will be posted on our social media accounts before the event, so if you are interested, keep an eye out for the event!
