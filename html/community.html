Controversy of 《Everything Everywhere All at Once》
The film has just been released in Taiwan, but the translation of the subtitles has accidentally led to a firestorm and a debate. The controversy caused by the translation has forced the film's distributor to apologise.
Many people felt that the film was superb but the translation was a mess, but the translator stressed that "there is no right or wrong translation, only whether the boss likes it or not" and that "my own translation is good".
But this attitude seems to have upset many people who were looking forward to the film and even said they would refuse to see it in cinemas.
