our club's opinion
For our club, film is a combination of moving pictures and slide projection to create a continuous image, a visual and auditory synthesis of modern technology and art.
SIGNIFICANCE OF FILM

"The significance of film is that it only makes the world appear more real to your eyes, without the masking and covering of shame, allowing the perspective of people to expand more far-reaching and wide, enriching the viewer's thinking perspective, allowing them to intersect with the original embedded ideas and concepts, and reap new enlightenment."

FEATURED

"In film we can see landscapes that haunt us and make our hearts grow fonder, we can peek into other people's lives, we can touch things that we would never be able to touch in our lifetime."

As the seventh art, it is defined in a very broad sense. Film is a conglomeration of the first six arts, containing unique characteristics. What is film? I cannot define it precisely. But I would prefer that you define your own film with your own perceptions.

PURPOSE OF FILM

A good film gives you a more open-eyed view and makes you think more with your head, rather than just for visual pleasure. Philosophy in film is more interesting than books and more lucid than life.

Film has allowed mankind to live nearly three times longer, and it has taught many truths that one could never understand in a lifetime. Look at life from a different perspective, accept the reality that you are ultimately ordinary, but never give up the pursuit of your ideals, be kind-hearted, love life and enjoy the moment.

Perhaps, by understanding this, you have come to understand the true meaning of life.

OUR EVENT PURPOSE

The aim of our event is that we hope all viewers will learn the following from the story.

  • One, to look at other people's stories and experience life differently.
  • Secondly, to ponder the thoughts of the creators and think about their social significance.
  • Thirdly, to appreciate art and to delight the mind and body.
