WEEKLY MOVIE NIGHTS
On 2019, we started a movie night through a community platform on the internet, all fixed at 9pm every Saturday, on the platform discord. It has been about 80 weeks since then, with a total of 75 films shown across four main themes. These are science fiction, drama, comedy and history.

The response to our campaign has been extraordinary. Our movie nights are aimed not only at our students but also at the general public. Anyone who is interested in film can take part in any of our events.
