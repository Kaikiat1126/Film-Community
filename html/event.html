WHAT IS FILM TO US?
The word film is commonly and unfamiliarly understood by the general public. Many people think that a film is just a two-hour drama with a flashy plot and visuals that are mostly filled with special effects and super expensive productions to manipulate the viewer's emotions. However, this is not the case.
Films can be based on great storylines or remakes of real-life cases. Often, a series of social stories and moral values are reflected behind the scenes, and bring the darker side of our society is brought to the public's attention, and the reality of society is examined together.
Of course, the social stories brought out in the films may also subconsciously influence the minds of the viewers. The storylines in the films are both beautiful and cruel, but there is no denying that there is some meaning behind the stories. Changing the universal values of the viewer is perhaps the meaning and value of a film.
PREVIOUS EVENT
FILM DAY

A major event, the Film Day, was held on campus.

Several excellent movies were provided for teachers and students on the day, and five lecture classrooms were rented for the screening of the films, with 350 people attending. The event lasted for 6 hours and 10 films were shown, including "The Redemption of Schenker", "LEON" and else.

The feedback from the event was unanimously positive, but due to the pandemic it was not possible to hold any major gatherings. We hope to bring the festival back to APU campus life again in the near future.

TALK “What Film Brings to Us?”

Held a major talk on campus, "What Film Brings to Us?”.

The whole talk took two hours and was conducted in an interactive format with the audience. The talk will focus on the benefits of watching films, why we should watch films, what films bring to our lives, how we can be inspired by a film, and other topics.

The venue was packed to the rafters with participants. It was a great honour to be able to present the topics that inspired many of the audience and the support of all was greatly appreciated.

AVENGERS KEYCHAIN WORKSHOP

A month after the release of Avengers 4: Endgame, we teamed up with Woodworking Workshop to host a wooden key ring workshop. Apart from rubbing off on the movie, it was mainly a great experience to be inspired to make hands-on products around the movie.

The event received a great response from teachers and students on campus and we look forward to organising similar workshops in the near future.
